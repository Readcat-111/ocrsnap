Screen Scanner — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Screen Scanner is built to respect your privacy. The short version: your images and extracted text never leave your PC, and we don't collect anything about you.

What we collect

Nothing. Screen Scanner has no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics or telemetry. We do not collect, transmit, store, or sell any personal information.

How your data is handled

  • Screen captures & images you scan are processed entirely on your device using on-device OCR (Windows' built-in OCR). They are never uploaded to us or anyone else.
  • Extracted text is placed on your Windows clipboard (so you can paste it) and, if you choose, saved to your local scan history. This history lives only in your app-data folder on your PC and is never transmitted.
  • Clipboard access is used only when you ask Screen Scanner to scan a copied image, and only to read that image and to write the recognized text back for you.
  • Exports (TXT/PDF) are saved to the location you choose on your PC.

Network use

Screen Scanner works fully offline for the free tier. It makes no network requests unless you use the optional AI OCR feature, which sends the image you're scanning to the configured cloud AI provider for processing.

Children's privacy

Screen Scanner does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If a future version adds an optional cloud-powered feature (for example, AI recognition), it will be clearly labelled, strictly opt-in, and covered by an updated version of this policy before it processes anything off your device.
